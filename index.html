Empowering schools and communities with digital tools and training.

What is the Mobile Hub all about?

Our Mobile Hub is adynamic and mobile digital learning facility equipped with laptops, internet connectivity, and tailored educational resources.

Opportunities for schools and communities

The mobile hub offers:

  • Digital Literacy Training: Basic to advanced digital skills development.
  • Online safety education: Promoting safe and responsibile internet use.
  • STEM Workshops: Engaging students in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics activities.
  • Income Generation Skills: Training for Almajiri students on creating digital content for economic opportunities.

How does it works?

1. Book a session: Schools and community groups can easily book a session through our online booking form.

2. Onsite Setup: Our team will arrive at your location with all the necessary equipment, including laptops and internet access.

3. Facilitated Learning: Our experienced trainers will guide participants through digital literacy sessions, tailored to meet your specific needs.

4. Learning and Engagement: Participants will gain practical experience with digital tools, enhanced their online safety awareness, and explore creative and educational opportunities using technology.

Terms & Conditions

  • Availability: The Mobile Hub is currently availiable only within Bauchi metropolis.
  • Security Measures: We prioritize safety for our equipment, team, and participants. The booking party is expected to provide a secure and conducive learning environment.
  • Booking Confirmations: All bookings are subject to comfirmation based on availability.
  • Cancellations: Please notify us atleast 48hours in advance for any cancellations or rescheduling.
